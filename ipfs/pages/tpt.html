Pre-Mining
 
Pre-Mining runs for 14 Days from 11 November 2020 12:05 UTC to 25 November 2020 11:59 UTC
 
RULES
 
Stake +50 TRX (minimum) on any prediction to mine and earn +10 TPT
 
Formula: STAKE (TRX) / 5 = TPT EARNED
Example: 100 TRX (STAKE) / 5 = 20 TPT (EARNED)
Mining
 
Mining starts from 25 November 2020 12:05 UTC
 
RULES
 
Stake any TRX amount on any prediction to mine and earn TPT.
TPT mined and earned is based on a daily "divisible", which increases everyday.
The divisible increases by 50 each day.
 
Formula: STAKE (TRX) / DIVISIBLE = TPT EARNED
Example: 300 TRX (STAKE) / 100 (DIVISIBLE) = 3 TPT (EARNED)
